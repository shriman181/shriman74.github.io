The Gap

Hydrogen infrastructure
is being built blind.

Billions are flowing into electrolysers, pipelines, and storage. Yet operators run on spreadsheets, gut feel, and manual inspections. There is no software stack designed for this industry's specific risks and workflows.

⚡

Electrolyser Degradation is Invisible

Stack efficiency decays silently. Without real-time monitoring and predictive diagnostics, operators discover faults only after costly production losses.

🔍

Leaks Are a Safety & Cost Crisis

Hydrogen molecules are the smallest in existence. Distribution network leakage is endemic, undetected for hours, and carries catastrophic safety implications.

📋

Green Certification Has No Infrastructure

Buyers, regulators, and carbon markets need certified green hydrogen provenance. Today there is no trusted, auditable traceability system at scale.

How It Works

From raw sensor data
to certified insight.

Nuveda integrates at the sensor and SCADA layer — no rip-and-replace. We sit above your existing hardware and deliver intelligence.

01

Connect

Integrate via OPC-UA, Modbus, REST, or our edge agent. Works with existing SCADA, DCS, and IoT sensors from all major vendors.

02

Ingest & Normalise

Multi-source telemetry — pressure, flow, temperature, purity, energy consumption — unified into a hydrogen-specific data model in real time.

03

Analyse & Predict

Domain-trained ML models run anomaly detection, degradation forecasting, leak probability scoring, and safety risk assessment continuously.

04

Act & Certify

Actionable alerts delivered to operators. Green hydrogen production automatically logged, certified, and made available for regulatory audit.

Why Now

Infrastructure first.
Software second.
We're second.

The global hydrogen build-out is happening now. The hardware is being deployed. But the operational software layer doesn't exist yet. This is the exact moment to establish the category.

Electrolyser capacity grew 5× in 3 years

From ~10GW in 2022 to 50GW+ by 2025. Hundreds of facilities now need operational software to run efficiently.

Green certification is becoming mandatory

EU's Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism (CBAM) and hydrogen standards require provenance proof. Compliance software is now non-optional.

Safety incidents are multiplying with scale

More facilities means more risk exposure. Regulators globally are tightening hydrogen safety standards, creating software mandates.

Zero incumbent software vendors

There is no Palantir, Aveva, or Aspen for hydrogen. The category is wide open for a purpose-built platform to define the standard.

Founder

Built by someone who knows where the bodies are buried.

Mari Ganesh Kumar founded Nuveda with a single conviction: the hydrogen transition will succeed or fail on the quality of its operational software — and right now, that software doesn't exist.

With a background spanning industrial IoT, energy systems, and machine learning, Mari has spent years at the intersection of physical infrastructure and data intelligence. He has worked closely with energy operators, witnessed first-hand the fragility of current monitoring practices, and identified the fundamental gap in the hydrogen software stack.

Nuveda is his answer to that gap — a platform built not by software generalists parachuted into energy, but by someone who understands electrolyser physics, pipeline fluid dynamics, and the regulatory realities of green hydrogen certification from first principles.

"The infrastructure is being welded together right now. Every week that passes without intelligent software is a week of blind operation, missed efficiency, and undetected risk. We're here to change that."
